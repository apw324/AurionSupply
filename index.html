Ward Mediterranean

Authentic Tunisian Olive Oil, Crafted with Tradition

Ward Mediterranean imports high-quality extra virgin olive oil sourced from family-owned groves across Tunisia. We focus on authenticity, sustainability, and flavor — bringing the warmth of the Mediterranean to kitchens across America. Each batch is carefully selected, cold-pressed, and bottled with care to preserve its natural richness and aroma.
We are a Mediterranean olive oil mill from Tunisia, positioned at the Premium Extra Virgin level (acidity less than 0.50%, peroxide value less than 10...) and even ultra-premium (medal-winning batch: acidity less than or equal to 0.30%, peroxide value 5, high polyphenol content, fruity, spicy, and slightly bitter).
